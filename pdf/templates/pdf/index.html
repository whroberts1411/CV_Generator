Create an online CV

This site provides a number of options for handling your contact details, personal educational and employment history, and for recording all your relevant job skills.

  • Go to the 'Enter Details' page from the menu bar at the top of the screen. From here, you can enter all your contact details and other details that you want to be included on your CV. Once you are happy with the information, your CV will be stored on an internal database.

  • To browse through all the CVs that are stored on the database, go to the 'CV List' option from the menu bar. This screen will display a list of available CVs (just the name and a few details), with options to either create a pdf version of the CV, or display it for editing.

  • Depending on the size of your CV, producing the final pdf file and downloading it may take a few seconds, but you'll be notified when it has completed. Your browser settings will determine what happens to the pdf CV after it has been produced. For example, Firefox may display a dialogue box giving the options to save or display the file. Other browsers may just download it to your local machine automatically. It's all down to the way you've configured your browser...

  • You're not limited to storing a single CV - you can create and store as many as you like. You could have one for your main area of employment, and another for charity work that you do, for example.
